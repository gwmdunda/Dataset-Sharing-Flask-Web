Introduction

Data Owner

As a data owner, you can obtain data sources from different people/organizations. Also, you can outsource people to curate the received data. The final curated dataset is available to the public for the price you set.

Data Collaborator

As a data collaborator, you can submit the data into our database as a collector user. You can earn money based on number of curated data you submit and how many users download

Data Curator

To ensure a good quality of the submitted data, you can also get paid by curating it. You can use the base tools in the website or use your own.

Data Explorer

We provide millions of available dataset with various use cases and types. You can explore millions of dataset in our platform.
